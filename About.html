About Us




Turitor is an Outstanding
Kindergarten in cambridge, for pre-school
and nursery-age children


We are professionals in the craft of caring for, and developing,
vibrant children who feel confident in their own identity,
who look at the world as an opportunity for rich discovery through trying, growing and exploring.







  • We only employ qualified professional staff.

  • We promise to care for your child’s individual
    requirements.

  • High staff to child ratio ensures your child
    receives lots of care and attention.

  • Total security throughout with coded entrance
    locks and CCTV cameras to all areas.

  • Individual children's play areas with soft surfacing.


Our Mission



We’re more than educators. We help families build
confidence for life. Our programs give children a
foundation they can build on. Wherever they go
after they leave Turitor, they’ll be ready to think
bigger, feel stronger, and aim higher.
